# Case A – Discipline-Optics Founder (Anonymized)

This is a generic pattern illustration. Any resemblance to real individuals or companies is coincidental.

## 1. Narrative Profile
- Strong focus on discipline, motivation, and self-improvement.
- Claims to be on a mission to help a vulnerable group (e.g., small workers, freelancers).
- Heavy use of short quotes, slogans, and binary cultural critiques.

## 2. Structural Data (Abstracted)
- Multi-year operating losses.
- Negative unit economics; no clear path to profitability.
- Market is under pressure from AI automation and global competitors.

## 3. TSAF Metrics (Illustrative)
- R ≈ 1.5 (binary logic, limited system modeling).
- High OSR (optics far exceed substance).
- High CS (discipline narrative vs repeated financial undiscipline).
- High MR (macro / AI exposure).
- SV in collapse zone; Pc(T) rises rapidly over 3–5 years.

## 4. Lessons
- Discipline at the individual level does not substitute for system design.
- Mission language can invert into harm if the platform is structurally unstable.
